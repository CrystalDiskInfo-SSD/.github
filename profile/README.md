## What is CrystalDiskInfo?

**CrystalDiskInfo** is a comprehensive and reliable hard drive monitoring utility designed to provide detailed information about the health status of your HDDs and SSDs. Using industry-standard **S.M.A.R.T. technology**, it continuously monitors various disk parameters including temperature, performance metrics, error rates, and overall drive health. The software offers real-time alerts and warnings when potential issues are detected, helping users prevent data loss and drive failures before they occur.

With its user-friendly interface and powerful diagnostic capabilities, CrystalDiskInfo displays crucial information such as power-on hours, spin-up time, read error rates, and reallocated sectors. It supports a wide range of storage devices including traditional hard drives, solid-state drives, and even NVMe drives. The software's color-coded health status system makes it easy to identify potential problems at a glance, while its detailed technical information satisfies advanced users and IT professionals.

CrystalDiskInfo's proactive monitoring approach allows users to maintain their storage devices effectively, extending their lifespan and ensuring data integrity. Whether you're a casual user concerned about your system's storage health or a professional managing multiple drives, this tool provides essential insights into your storage devices' condition and performance metrics.
